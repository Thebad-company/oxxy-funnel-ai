# Design System: Oxxy Official (Sync with oxxy.in)
**Project ID:** 9489081948777624416

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The "Oxxy Official" design system is **"Modern Healthcare Professional."** 
It moves away from editorial warmth toward a clean, tech-first, and trust-driven medical aesthetic. The theme pairs the approachability of **Theme Teal** with the established authority of **Theme Dark Blue**. The interface is characterized by generous white space, clear hierarchies, and rounded interactive elements.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
*   **Theme Teal (#03A297):** Primary brand color. Used for primary CTAs, active states, and scan markers.
*   **Theme Dark Blue (#023350):** Secondary brand color. Used for headings, footer backgrounds, and heavy UI components.
*   **Neutral Background (#F5F7FA):** Light gray-blue used for section contrast and secondary surfaces.
*   **Muted Body Text (#757F95):** Used for long-form paragraphs to reduce visual strain.
*   **Pure Surface (#FFFFFF):** Core card and container background.

## 3. Typography Rules
*   **Headlines (Inter):** A modern, geometric sans-serif that conveys technical precision. Used for all primary and secondary headings.
*   **Body Text (Roboto):** A highly legible sans-serif used for all functional text, labels, and paragraph content.

## 4. Component Stylings
*   **Buttons:** Prominent `10px` border-radius. Primary buttons use Theme Teal gradient/solid.
*   **Cards/Containers:** Soft `12px` to `16px` rounding. Subtle shadows (Ambient) on pure white backgrounds.
*   **Scan Module:** The scan-line and progress indicator should now use the Theme Teal (#03A297) for consistency with the brand's diagnostic identity.

## 5. Layout Principles
*   **Vertical Padding:** Generous spacing (`100px`+) between major sections.
*   **Alignment:** Clean, grid-aligned layouts with centered primary content for high focus.
